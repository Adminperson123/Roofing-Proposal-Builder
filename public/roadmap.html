What it does

14 production-grade features

Each card below is a deployed, customer-touching capability — not a roadmap promise.

🧠

AI tier generation

GPT-4 Turbo writes 3 polished package options (Essential / Performance / Signature) tailored to the customer's exact scope, in seconds.

👁️

AI roof analysis

GPT-4o reads photos the rep snaps on-site and pre-fills squares, pitch, material guess, condition score, and recommended add-ons.

💰

Live pricing engine

Per-square shingle/tile pricing, automatic pitch / story / layer adders, solar per-panel rate, manual price overrides, and tier-visibility control.

✨

Customer-side upgrades

The customer toggles optional change orders directly on the proposal page; the price recalculates live and folds into their final signed total.

📨

Multi-channel send

One modal sends an editable text message + email through GoHighLevel — including an EN/ES language toggle powered by OpenAI translation.

✍️

E-sign on the page

Finger-on-glass signature captured, stamped with IP + timestamp, and locked into the proposal. No DocuSign round-trip.

📅

Project timeline

Each customer gets a private /c/[token] link showing where their roof project stands — milestones the rep edits live.

📄

5-page narrative PDF

Auto-generated PDF that mirrors the web proposal: cover, about, families, materials, packages, benefits, cost of waiting, payment schedule.

🖥️

Presentation mode

14-slide deck the rep walks the customer through in-home — swipe, keyboard, and tap nav, optimized for phone/tablet.

🔁

48-hour follow-up

If the customer viewed but didn't sign, a friendly nudge SMS goes out automatically on the second day — once, never twice.

🤖

AI proposal editor

The rep types "drop Best 5% and swap the rep to David" in plain English; the AI translates that into structured edits + a versioned save.

🔗

GHL CRM sync

Contacts, custom fields, tags, and opportunity stages flow into GoHighLevel automatically when a proposal is sent, viewed, or signed.

🔍

Site inspection module

Step-by-step on-site inspection separate from the proposal flow, with photo capture and a generated report PDF.

📊

Owner dashboard

Live KPIs — revenue closed, conversion %, open pipeline, hot leads, rep leaderboard, accepted tier mix, and a live activity feed.

What's next

Open items — by priority

Bucketed by urgency. The high-priority items are things only you (Oscar) or Beltran can do — they need credentials or business decisions, not more code.

🔴 Rotate ADMIN_PASSWORD in Vercel

The previous value lives in git history. Set a fresh one in Vercel env vars and share with the team. One-line click in Vercel project settings.

🔴 Set solar per-panel rate

Confirm the rate with the client, then enter it in Settings → Add-On Prices. The per-panel pricing mechanism is dormant at $0 and falls back to flat pricing until set.

🔴 Tighten public-API allow-list

Convert redact() in /api/proposal/[id] to an explicit allow-list of customer-facing fields so internal rep notes and AI cost math don't leak with the proposal URL.

🟡 Lock the GHL webhook

Add a shared-secret bearer or HMAC to /api/webhook so untrusted text can't enter the AI prompt pipeline. Requires coordinating the secret on the GHL side.

🟡 Rate-limit critical endpoints

Add @upstash/ratelimit to /api/auth/login (brute-force protection) and /api/proposal/[id]/accept/view (rep-SMS spam protection + OpenAI cost protection).

🟡 Load change-orders starter list

In Settings → Change Orders, click "Load starter list" and price each item. They don't appear to customers on /p/[id] until priced above $0.

🟢 Make proposal-photos bucket private

Switch storage bucket to private + serve via signed URLs gated by the proposal-id check. Closes the photo-enumeration vector.

🟢 Vercel Hobby → Pro

$20/mo unlocks hourly cron (current daily window means follow-up SMS can fire 36–96h after view, not the ideal 48–72h band).
